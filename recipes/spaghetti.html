Spaghetti

This is a classic Swiss dish. It gained its reputation because of the very high calorie in a very easy to make dish. Because this dish was used in country heavy on farmers, it became increasingly popular. One meal in the morning fueled the farmers till noon.

Ingredients:

  • Potato
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Boil the potatoes
  2. Leave the potatoes in the fridge for one night
  3. Grate the potatoes
  4. Heat pan with olive oil
  5. Put butter in
  6. Put all the grated potato in the pan
  7. When needed, add a table spoon of milk for crust
  8. Keep the outside integrity, and let it turn brownish
  9. Remove and eat with cheese
